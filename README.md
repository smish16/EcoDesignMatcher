# 🌱 Eco-Design Matcher (UNSDG Goal 12 Prototype)

An AI-driven circular packaging engine and visual sync workspace built for independent, scaling Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) brands. This application allows users to optimize material choices, view logistics impacts, generate dynamic eco-branding slogans, and review dynamic packaging canvas mockups instantly.

---

## 📌 Project Overview & Context

The Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) brand ecosystem is experiencing exponential growth globally. However, this expansion has brought a massive surge in commercial packaging waste. Most emerging brands rely heavily on single-use plastics or generic multi-layered polymers because navigating the complex marketplace of sustainable materials is intensely confusing.

I selected **SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production)** because tackling waste at the manufacturing and distribution level yields a permanent downstream impact. By empowering small, independent businesses to systematically drop plastic configurations from day one, I aim to smoothly transition toward a localized circular economy. This addresses the critical deficit in resource optimization by making ecological product design accessible, cost-effective, and aesthetically unique.

---

## ✨ Features

* **AI Material Configuration Optimizer:** Maps individual product categories, unit weights, and barrier requirements against an abstracted logical matrix to output optimal biological alternatives.
* **Real-Time Lifecycle Diagnostics:** Instantly calculates estimated carbon emissions reduction percentages, dimensional package mass deltas, and structural stability ratings.
* **Dynamic Brand Palette Sync:** Integrates interactive primary and secondary color hex pickers directly into a real-time, custom packaging mockup layout container.
* **AI Environmental Copy Generation:** Dynamically delivers punchy, theme-tailored sustainable copy (e.g., *"There is no Planet B"*), optimized according to the brand's chosen visual persona.
* **Naive-User Directives:** Features an integrated, clear step-by-step instruction banner directly within the centered Hero header section to maximize ease of use.
